It depends on the service. A Basic Interior Clean takes around 1–1.5 hours, a Full Interior Detail takes 2.5–3 hours, and the Odour & Deep Clean can take 4–5 hours. Add-ons can add extra time too. We'll give you a more accurate estimate when we confirm your booking.
